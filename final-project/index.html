Córdoba Argentina Temple

4 October 2008 - Announced
30 October 2010 - Groundbreaking
17 May 2015 - Dedicated
The plans for the construction of the temple in Córdoba were announced during the general conference of the church on October 4, 2008 . 1 That same day the construction of the Rome Temple in Italy was announced; the Calgary Canada Temple; the Kansas City Temple ; and the Philadelphia temple , the latter two in the USA. 2 It is the second temple built in Argentina after the Buenos Aires Temple.
Following the announcement, a local church official indicated that the temple would be built in the Villa Belgrano neighborhood, next to the offices of the Córdoba Argentina Mission located in the city of Córdoba.
On October 30, 2010, the groundbreaking ceremony and the ecclesiastical dedication of the land on which the temple would be built were held, which was carried out by Neil L. Andersen who presided over the South America South area. Only local leaders and an additional 500 guests attended the groundbreaking ceremony.
