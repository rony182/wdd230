Target Audience

The target audience is described bellow.
Who: CEO's, Business owners, Business Stakeholders and others interested in getting to know better the group, be part of it and follow news and events.
Age: 25 to 90.
Education: People who are graduated and have a promising future on their careers, and people who had achieved their goals and want to help others.
Income: Income levels will typicaly be between $85,000 and above annually.
Motivation: Be part of a selected group or stay updated about their activities and events that the group will be holding and sponsoring.
